Installation Steps:

  • Install the Blind Under Your Eaves
    Begin by installing the blind under your eaves.
  • Assemble the Fence Mounts
    Take each pair of parts and simply insert them together to assemble the fence mounts.
  • Attach the Fence Mount to the Blind
    Insert the assembled fence mount (as shown in the photo) into the bottom of the blind. You may need to remove the end cap temporarily to do this.
  • Repeat for the Remaining Fence Mounts
    Repeat this process for the remaining three fence mounts.
  • Hook the First Mount onto the Fence
    Position the first fence mount onto the fence and lightly hook it in place to keep it from falling off.
  • Snap in the First Mount
    Once the first mount is hooked in place, snap the two parts of the first fence mount together to secure it.
  • Repeat above 2 steps for the Remaining Mounts
    For the second, third, and fourth fence mounts, repeat the steps, lightly hook each one onto the fence, then snap in the two parts for each mount. Do this for all four mounts.
  • Adjust the Tension of the Blind
    Use the wand to adjust the tension of the blind to ensure it is taut.
  • Completed
    Your installation is now complete—enjoy your shaded space!
